Our Process

Our process is simple and transparent:

  1. 50% deposit before starting the project.
  2. We work closely with you to create the best website for your needs.
  3. The remaining balance is due upon completion, before the website goes live.
  4. If you need a domain, we can arrange that for you, which will affect the final price.
  5. Maintenance packages are optional and can be added later.
